Welcome to ProjectOK!

This website exists in order to help anyone who is in a program development team to stay organized and on track when it comes to whatever they are in the progress of creating. No more wondering when or where those annoying meetings are and no longer do you need to scarmble through your emails in order to try and find out how to get into contact with a team member of yours. Here, everything can be at the tips of your fingers.

However, don't worry about making your project all that and extraordinary! Here, it is okay to be simply OK, though you can strive to be more, we don't expect much more than averageness from you.

How does it work, you might be wondering? Well, click that cool little login button and join in on the fun today!
